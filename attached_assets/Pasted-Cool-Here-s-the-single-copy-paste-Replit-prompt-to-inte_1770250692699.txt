### Two quick setup tips so this works smoothly

* In PandaDoc, build your template with tokens like `{{loan_amount}}`, `{{interest_rate}}`, `{{borrower_name}}`, etc.
* For signature blocks, set roles like **Borrower**, **Originator**, etc. PandaDoc will handle placement.
